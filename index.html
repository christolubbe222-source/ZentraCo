The real questions, answered straight.

/01What exactly do you build?
Whatever gets the phone to ring. Every engagement is shaped to the business. That usually includes a new storefront, an intake system, AI follow-up, and paid marketing, but the exact parts depend on where the business is bleeding leads today. We tell you on the diagnosis call.
/02How long does it take?
Most builds go live in about two weeks from the green light. We move fast because the systems are sharp and specific, not because we cut corners.
/03Do you work with companies outside the trades?
Sometimes, if the business has the same shape. Service-driven, appointment-led, local-first. If that sounds like you, book a call and we'll tell you honestly.
/04What about after launch?
We stay. Retainers are optional but common. The system is monitored, tuned, and quietly adjusted as the business grows or the market shifts.
/05Do you take on everyone who asks?
No. We're selective. If we're not the right fit, we'll tell you on the first call and point you somewhere better.
