The real questions, answered straight.

/01What does an AI receptionist do for an electrician's business?
An AI receptionist answers every inbound call within seconds, qualifies the caller against your services and pricing, and books jobs directly into your dispatch calendar without any human involvement. For electricians, this means emergency calls get captured at 11pm, overflow calls during peak periods never hit voicemail, and your team only handles conversations that genuinely need them. You do not add headcount. you just stop losing calls.
/02What is included in a Zentra Co website build?
Every build includes a custom-designed, conversion-first website written specifically for your trade, mobile-first performance, local SEO scaffolding, and trust signals built around how homeowners actually decide. We also wire in your intake flows: contact forms, quote requests, and booking paths, so the site actively pulls leads into your pipeline, not just presents information. Nothing is templated.
/03How quickly can an AI system respond to a new lead?
The system responds within 60 seconds of a lead coming in, regardless of the time or day. Research shows leads contacted within the first minute convert at up to 7x the rate of leads contacted an hour later. For solar companies and electricians where multiple contractors are often quoting the same job, being first is often the whole game.
/04What happens to leads that come in after hours?
After-hours leads are handled the same way as any other. The AI receptionist answers the call, qualifies the caller, and books the job if it is straightforward, or sends you a prioritized text summary for jobs that need a human decision in the morning. Nothing sits in an inbox until Monday.
/05How long does it take to set up the Zentra Co system?
Most builds go live in about two weeks from the start date. The first week covers the website design, copy, and structure. The second week is the intake layer: AI receptionist, automations, CRM connections, and testing. We move fast because the system is specific and proven, not because we cut corners.
/06How is Zentra Co different from a regular marketing agency?
Most agencies run ads or build a page and report on impressions. We build and operate the system that turns those impressions into booked jobs: the AI receptionist, the follow-up sequences, the intake flows, and the infrastructure that makes sure traffic actually converts. For electricians and solar companies, the gap between a lead arriving and a job being booked is where most revenue is lost. That is the gap we close.
