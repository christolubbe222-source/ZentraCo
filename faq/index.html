FAQ

The real questions,
answered straight.

Everything you need to know about how we work, what we build, and what to expect before you book a call.

/01What exactly does Zentra Co build?
We build the full intake system for your trade business. That means a conversion-first website, an AI receptionist that answers calls 24/7, automated follow-up sequences, and the integrations that connect everything to your calendar and CRM. The exact scope depends on where your business is bleeding leads — we diagnose that on the first call.
/02How long does a build take?
Most builds go live in about two weeks from green light. We move fast because we work from a specific, tested system — not a blank canvas every time. Complexity and integrations can add time, but two weeks is our standard.
/03What industries do you work with?
Primarily electricians, solar contractors, and roofers. We occasionally work with other service businesses that are appointment-led and local-first. If you're not in those trades, book a call and we'll tell you honestly whether we're the right fit.
/04How does an AI receptionist work for a trade business?
The AI receptionist answers your phone in seconds, qualifies the caller using your scripts, books jobs straight into your calendar for straightforward requests, and routes anything complex to you via text or call. It handles after-hours volume, overflow, and repeat callers without adding headcount.
/05What happens after the build goes live?
We don't disappear at launch. We monitor the system, tune the intake flows, update scripts as your business changes, and run monthly reviews of what's converting. Ongoing retainers are optional but most clients stay on.
/06Do you run paid ads as well?
Yes. Our Operator package includes Google Ads, Meta campaigns, local SEO, and landing pages. We only run ads once the intake system is live — there's no point driving traffic to a pipeline that leaks.
/07What does it cost?
Pricing depends on what you need built. Every engagement starts with a free diagnosis call where we map what's actually bleeding leads. From there we quote a specific scope. We don't publish rates because cookie-cutter pricing produces cookie-cutter results.
/08How is Zentra Co different from a regular web agency?
Most agencies build a page and hand you the keys. We build and operate the system underneath — the AI that answers calls at 11pm, the follow-up that texts a quote within 60 seconds, the pipeline that doesn't leak when you're busy. The website is just the front door.
