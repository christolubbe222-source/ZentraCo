Questions solar pros ask before they spend.

/01How much should a solar company budget for Google Ads?
Ad spend varies by market and competition, but solar campaigns typically need more budget than other trades because of higher cost-per-click. We size the budget around your install area and capacity, then scale it once we know the real cost per signed install.
/02What's the difference between Meta ads and just boosting a post?
A boosted post reaches more people who already follow you. A Meta ad campaign targets homeowners in your install area who don't, using lead forms, retargeting, and creative built around solar incentives, tracked back to signed installs rather than likes.
/03Do I need a new website before running ads?
It helps. Ads send traffic to a page, and a slow or dated site wastes that spend. If your site isn't converting, this pairs with our website design and development service, or we can build dedicated landing pages as part of your ad campaigns.
/04How fast will I see results from Google or Meta ads for solar?
Search campaigns can generate appointment requests within days of launch. Because solar has a longer sales cycle, the first 30 days focus on gathering signal and cutting waste, then retargeting and email nurture compound results as quotes move toward signing.
/05What does the retargeting, email and SMS follow-up actually do?
Solar decisions take weeks. Retargeting ads keep your name in front of quote-shoppers, automated multi-week email sequences answer the objections that stall a decision, SMS reminders catch homeowners when they're ready, and old quotes get reactivated automatically.
/06Do you handle SEO for solar companies too?
Yes, but as its own dedicated service. This page covers paid ads, retargeting, and tracking. For Google Business Profile, local rankings, and organic search, see our SEO for Solar Pro's service. Most clients run both side by side.
