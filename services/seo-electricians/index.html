Questions electricians ask about SEO.

/01How long does SEO take to start working for an electrician?
Most electricians see initial movement in local rankings within 60 to 90 days, with Google Business Profile changes often showing the fastest impact. Meaningful organic lead flow typically builds over 4 to 6 months as on-page, citation, and content work compounds.
/02What's included in Google Business Profile optimization?
Full profile setup or cleanup, category and service selection, photo and post scheduling, Q&A management, and ongoing monitoring, all tuned to rank in the local map pack for your service area.
/03Do I need a new website for SEO to work?
Not necessarily. We can optimize an existing site's structure, speed, and content. That said, sites built on Zentra Co's conversion-first framework include SEO foundations from day one, so pairing this with our website design and development service compounds faster.
/04What's the difference between SEO and paid ads?
Ads stop the moment you stop paying. SEO is a compounding asset: rankings built over months keep generating calls with no per-click cost. Most electricians run both: ads for immediate volume, SEO for the long-term asset that lowers cost per lead over time.
/05Do you build separate pages for each service area?
Yes. Electricians often cover multiple cities or suburbs, and each one gets its own optimized page targeting the specific terms homeowners in that area search, rather than one generic page trying to rank everywhere.
/06Do you handle Google Ads and Meta ads too?
Yes, as a separate dedicated service. This page covers organic rankings, Google Business Profile, and local search. For paid traffic, retargeting, and tracked ROI, see our Ads & Marketing for Electricians service. Most clients run both side by side.
